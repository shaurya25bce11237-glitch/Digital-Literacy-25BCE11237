Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

DO'S

1) Set up unique passwords on all accounts with capital and lower-case letters, different characters and numbers. 

2) Use Two-Factor Authentication for Email, Banking Apps and Social Media. 

3) Always check the sender name or email address and links in emails, messages and adverts before clicking. 

4) Keep phones, apps and anti-virus software regularly updated. 

5) Only use official apps from legit stores like Google Play Store or App Store

6) Check that the URL begins with https before entering any Login / Payment details. 

7) Frequently check your bank statements or transactions on your UPI.

8) Always log out of accounts when using shared or public equipment.

DON'TS

1) Never divulge your OTP, PIN, Password or CVV for your card details to anyone - even if they are claiming to be from your bank.

2) Do NOT Scan QR Codes you do not recognise (you may be transferring money by doing so rather than receiving.)

3) Do NOT click on any links that appear suspicious i.e. offering work or prize or urgent action for an account.

4) Avoid using Public Wi-Fi to perform any Banking or Financial Transactions.

5) Do NOT download any attachments from unknown sources or untrusted sources

6) Avoid Excessively sharing personal details or information e.g. your residential address, mobile number, National ID Number. 

7) Never trust any unknown phone number that provide customer care assistance without verification.

8) Do NOT save sensitive passwords on Shared Equipment or Browsers.
