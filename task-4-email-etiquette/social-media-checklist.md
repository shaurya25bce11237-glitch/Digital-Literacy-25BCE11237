**Social Media Guidelines:**

**> DOs**

**- Evaluate content before posting for appropriateness and respectfulness.**

**- Practice good privacy settings to protect your personal information.**

**- Use professional language on networks such as LinkedIn.**

**- Verify accuracy of information you plan to share to avoid spreading misinformation.**

**- Use respect and courteousness in your comments and discussions with others.**



**> DON’Ts**

**- Do not post personal information such as telephone numbers, addresses, or passwords.**

**- Avoid posting inappropriate, offensive, or abusive content.**

**- Avoid participating in online arguments or cyberbullying.**

**- Avoid giving too much information about your daily life or sharing sensitive documents/information.**

**- Avoid opening links from sources that seem suspicious to you.**
