Hello and welcome! My name is Shaurya Gupta; I am proud to announce my selection as a Student Digital Ambassador for VIT Bhopal. I look forward to assisting my peers in learning how digital tools can create a successful online presence and how to use them securely!

The information in this repository represents the Digital Literacy Portfolio of all five key areas I have worked on in my time here at VIT Bhopal. I'm sharing this digital portfolio so the committee members can better understand my digital literacy development, as well as provide my fellow batch members with a framework for navigating today’s society digitally.

Mission Statement
To create a bridge between technical skill and digital responsibility so that all students are able to navigate the global internet with confidence, safety and professionalism.

Projects Breakdown
Project 1: Visual Reference
I constructed a visual roadmap of digital literacy in which complicated safe internet use rules are made to be "scannable" and accessible at a glance.

Reflection: I learned how to apply visual hierarchy in order to highlight pertinent safety information.

Project 2: Professional Foundation
I built my professional foundation upon a triad of platforms, which serve as documentation of the four years I have completed in CSE Core and will be useful for finding employment.

GitHub: in which to store code, and collaborate with others

LinkedIn: where I can develop a networking system, and connect with my peers that graduated from VIT

StackOverflow: to connect with a large community of people to assist with solving problems

Project 3: Interactive Literacy
I created a Digital Literacy Quiz utilizing Google Forms so that my classmates could assess their own literacy. Furthermore, I demonstrated with HackerRank that the browser-based tools are all that an individual needs to begin practicing logic and coding.

Project 4: Responsible Communicator
As an Ambassador, I practiced using professional email etiquette by creating multiple scenarios for requesting academic extensions, and inquiring about internships, while placing emphasis on clarity, courtesy, and the "reply all" safety rule.

Project 5: Secure Citizen
I researched the increase in incidences of UPI fraud and scams when employing QR codes. The "Safe Habits" checklist was created as a part of my portfolio in order to provide my understands with protection from being the victim of financial fraud or being a target of an online phishing attack.
